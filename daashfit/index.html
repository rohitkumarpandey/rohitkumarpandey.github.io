About Us

DaashFit, the innovative mobile app developed by MightyBit Technologies Pvt Ltd. Our mission is to transform the landscape of gym based fitness in India by addressing the challenge of high costs that often hinder middle-class fitness enthusiasts from achieving their goals. Staggering need for an affordable and efficient fitness solution has never been more pressing.
